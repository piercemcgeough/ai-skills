---
name: grill-me
description: Interview the user relentlessly about a plan or design until reaching shared understanding, resolving each branch of the decision tree one by one. Use when the user says "grill me", "interview me", "let's plan", or "help me plan".
---

Interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this plan until we 
reach a shared understanding. Walk down each branch of the design 
tree resolving dependencies between decisions one by one.

If a question can be answered by exploring the project — files, code, 
documents, or any other project materials — explore it to drive the 
session forward, either by resolving answers directly or asking new 
relevant questions.

For each question, provide your recommended answer with a one-line 
reason.

When all major branches are resolved, produce a structured summary of 
decisions grouped by branch, flag a few optional areas worth exploring, 
and ask whether to go deeper or wrap up.
